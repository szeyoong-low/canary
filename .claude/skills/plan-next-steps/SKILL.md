## Steps
1. Read the pinned issue on the [Issues page](https://github.com/szeyoong-low/canary/issues) to see what feature needs to be built next.
1. Read the [Pull Requests page](https://github.com/szeyoong-low/canary/pulls) to understand what has been built.
2. Read [Architecture.md](../../../canary.wiki/Architecture.md) to get the big picture of the project.
3. Read the [Architecture Decision Records](../../../canary.wiki/architecture-decisions/) to review the rationale behind relevant decisions made so far.
4. Read the relevant files in the [frontend](../../../frontend/) and [backend](../../../backend/) directories.
5. Devise a detailed plan of action to complete the next feature on the roadmap and justify the architectural choices involved.
6. Discuss it with me according to the "Way of working" outlined in [CLAUDE.md](../../../CLAUDE.md).
7. After I agree, write up an [Architecture Decision Records](../../../canary.wiki/architecture-decisions/ADR-template.md) to document any significant architectural decisions made.
