## Steps
1. Read [PROGRESS.md](/PROGRESS.md) to understand what has been built, and what features need to
   be built to complete the phase.
2. Read [ARCHITECTURE.md](/ARCHITECTURE.md) to get the big picture of the project.
3. Read the [Architecture Decision Records](/ADR/) to review the rationale behind decisions made so far.
4. Read the relevant files in the [frontend](/frontend/) and [backend](/backend/) directories.
5. Devise a detailed plan of action to complete the next feature on the roadmap, and justify the architectural choices involved.
6. Discuss it with me according to the "Way of working" outlined in [CLAUDE.md](/CLAUDE.md).
7. After I agree, write up [Architecture Decision Records](/ADR/) to document any significant architectural decisions made.
