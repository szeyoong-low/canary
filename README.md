# 🦜 Canary
An agentic analyst that uncovers illuminating stories hidden in data about the
companies shaping our world.

> Lauch target: 17 July 2026

## Inspiration
Canary is inspired by the best storytellers in the business world. I'm a big fan
of [the Economist](https://www.economist.com/) and [the Acquired podcast](https://www.acquired.fm/).

When trying to use the [Bloomberg terminal](https://professional.bloomberg.com/products/bloomberg-terminal/),
I was surprised by how challenging it was to pull data to answer my curious
follow-up questions about the developments I read about in the news.

With Canary, newcomers to the business world can ask their burning questions and
let an AI agent take care of the rest. It fetches the right data and transform
it into an interactive chart along with a concise, ELI5 insight.
